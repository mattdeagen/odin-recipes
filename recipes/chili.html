Classic Chili

Description

A hearty and comforting classic chili made with ground beef, three types of beans, tomatoes, peppers, and a bold spice blend—perfect for game day or a cozy night in.

Ingredients

  • 1lb ground beef
  • 1 yellow onion
  • 1 red bell pepper
  • 1 green bell pepper
  • 3 jalapeños
  • 1 Tbsp minced garlic
  • 2 tsp cumin
  • 2 Tbsp chili powder
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 1 15oz can black beans
  • 1 15oz can pinto beans
  • 2 15oz cans kidney beans
  • 1 30oz can diced tomatoes
  • 1 30oz can tomato sauce
  • 1 10oz can diced tomatoes and green chiles

Instructions

  1. In a large skillet over medium-high heat, brown beef for about 4 minutes.
  2. Add diced onion, jalapeños, and bell peppers to the skillet and cook until tender.
  3. Add minced garlic and seasonings and cook for another 30 seconds.
  4. Transfer mixture to slow cooker and add drained cans of beans, diced tomatoes, and tomato sauce. Stir to combine.
  5. Cook on low for 8 hours or on high for 4 hours. Add salt to taste.
