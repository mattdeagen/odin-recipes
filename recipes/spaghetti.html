Spaghetti

Description

This classic spaghetti recipe features al dente pasta tossed in a savory tomato sauce with garlic, herbs, and a hint of sweetness. This dish is perfect for a quick yet satisfying meal the whole family will love.

Ingredients

  • 1lb ground beef
  • 1lb mild ground italian sausage
  • 1 yellow onion
  • 2 Tbsp minced garlic
  • 2 28oz cans crushed tomatoes
  • 1 29oz can tomato sauce
  • 1 6oz can tomato paste
  • 2 Tbsp italian seasoning
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 4 tsp brown sugar
  • 6 Tbsp butter
  • 2 tsp salt (or to taste)
  • 2lb uncooked spaghetti noodles
